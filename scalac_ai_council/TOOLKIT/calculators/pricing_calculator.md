## Price Increase Playbook

### When to Increase Prices
- [ ] 12+ months since last increase
- [ ] Inflation >5%
- [ ] Market rates increased
- [ ] Adding new services/capabilities
- [ ] Client renewal (existing relationship)

### How Much
- Existing clients: 5-10% per year
- New clients: Market rates
- Significant capability addition: 15-20%

