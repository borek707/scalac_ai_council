## Offer Launch Checklist (Marcus + Elena)

### Diagnosis Phase
- [ ] CORE vs AI diagnosis completed
- [ ] Klient ma infrastructure (jeśli AI)?
- [ ] Problem jest zdefiniowany (Gap Analysis)
- [ ] WTP (Willingness to Pay) zweryfikowane (Mom Test)

### Offer Package (Marcus)
- [ ] BrandScript ukończony
- [ ] Beachhead market zdefiniowany
- [ ] Good-Better-Best pricing gotowe

### Funnel Ready (Elena)
