# Example: Funnel Design - Team Extension (CORE)

## Context
Oferta: Team Extension dla fintechów Series B (z przykładu Marcusa)
Target: CTO/VP Engineering w fintechach EU
Deal size: 50-300k EUR
Sales cycle: 2-4 tygodnie

---

## 1. Funnel Design

### Stages & Conversions

```
1. Lead (Website visitor, LinkedIn, Referral)
   ↓ 15% conversion
2. MQL (Downloaded content, Requested Architecture Review)
   ↓ 30% conversion  
3. SQL (Had discovery call, identified pain)
   ↓ 40% conversion
4. Opportunity (Proposal sent, MEDDIC qualified)
   ↓ 35% conversion
5. Closed (Contract signed)
```

**Overall conversion:** Lead→Closed = 0.15 × 0.30 × 0.40 × 0.35 = 0.63%

### Volume Targets (Monthly)

| Stage | Volume | Conversion | Pipeline Value |
|-------|--------|------------|----------------|
| Leads | 300 | 15% | - |
| MQLs | 45 | 30% | - |
| SQLs | 14 | 40% | 420k EUR |
| Opps | 6 | 35% | 600k EUR |
| Closed | 2 | - | 200k EUR |

**Target:** 2 deals/month × 100k EUR = 200k PLN revenue/month

**Metrics:**
- Current team: 8 devs
- Target: 20 devs
- Hiring cost: 25k EUR/dev × 12 = 300k EUR
- Time to hire: 6 months
- Cost of delay: 500k EUR/month revenue impact

**Economic Buyer:**

- **Partially identified → Need CFO meeting**

**Decision Criteria:**
- Must have: Remote work OK, 2-week onboarding, Scala expertise

- **Clear criteria → Qualified**

**Decision Process:**
1. CTO approves vendor (1 week)

**Champion:**

**MEDDIC Score:** 5/6 qualified → **HOT OPPORTUNITY**

## 4. Growth Experiments

### Experiment 1: Pricing Presentation
**Hypothesis:** "Good-Better-Best" konwertuje lepiej niż custom pricing
**Channel:** CORE
**Test:** A/B test on proposal calls
- A: "Dostosujemy cenę do Waszych potrzeb"
- B: "Mamy trzy opcje: Good (85/h), Better (80/h), Best (75/h)"
**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Success:** >10% improvement in close rate

### Experiment 2: Architecture Review as PQL
**Hypothesis:** Darmowy Architecture Review generuje lepsze leady niż whitepaper
**Channel:** CORE
**Test:** 
- Landing page A: "Download Guide"
- Landing page B: "Free Architecture Review"
**Duration:** 6 weeks
**Success:** MQL→SQL conversion >25%

### Experiment 3: LinkedIn Video
**Hypothesis:** Personalized video (Loom) ma wyższy response rate niż text
**Channel:** Spears
**Test:** 
- A: Text LinkedIn message
- B: 60s Loom video
**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Success:** Response rate >20%

## 5. JOLT Framework (Overcoming Indecision)

### Problem: CTO nie może się zdecydować

**Indecision Diagnosis:**
- Lack of Information: NIE (ma wszystkie dane)
- Outcome Uncertainty: TAK ("Czy to zadziała?")
- Lack of Confidence: TAK ("Czy Scalac to dobry vendor?")
- Implementation Uncertainty: TAK ("Jak to wdrożyć?")

**JOLT Strategy:**

**J - Judge:** Medium indecision → JOLT needed

**O - Offer:**
"Dla Waszej sytuacji polecam zacząć od pakietu 'Better' - 3 devy na 3 miesiące. To daje Wam immediate capacity bez long-term commitment. Jak się sprawdzi - rozszerzamy."

**L - Limit:**
"Nie proponuję od razu 10 devów. Zacznijmy od 3, przetestujmy współpracę, potem skalujemy. To redukuje ryzyko do minimum."

**T - Take Risk Away:**
"2-week trial period. Jak dev nie pasuje - wymieniamy w 48h bez kosztów. Fixed price za onboarding - nie ma hidden costs."

**Result:** Indecision overcome → Deal closed

## 8. CORE vs AI Balance

**This is PURE CORE funnel:**
- No AI component
- Shorter cycle (2-4 weeks)
- Lower CAC (10k PLN)
- Higher volume (300 leads/month)
- Focus: Speed, reliability, scale

**AI Opportunity in this funnel:**
After 3-6 months współpracy:
- "Czy potrzebujecie AI skills w teamie?"
- Upsell do AI-capable team
