# Example 2: Funnel Design - Sovereign AI (AI)

## Funnel: Sovereign AI for Banking Compliance

### Type: AI

---

## Stages

| Stage | Definition | Conversion | Actions |
|-------|------------|------------|---------|
| **Lead** | Download content lub webinar signup | - | SEO, content, LinkedIn |
| **MQL** | Requested AI Compliance Assessment | 15% | Fast response, credibility |
| **SQL** | Paid assessment completed (5k EUR) | 40% | Deep dive, POC proposal |
| **POC** | POC in progress (3 months) | 60% | Weekly check-ins, demos |
| **Opportunity** | Production proposal sent | 50% | Business case, ROI |
| **Closed** | Production contract signed | 70% | Implementation plan |

**Overall conversion:** Lead → Closed = 1.26%

**Target:** 200 leads → 2-3 closed deals

---

## Three Pipelines

### Seeds (Inbound) - 40% of leads

**Channels:**
- SEO: "sovereign ai banking", "private llm compliance", "ai act preparation"
- Content: "The RAG Mirage", "Why 80% AI pilots fail"
- LinkedIn: Thought leadership (AI + compliance)

**Volume:** 80 leads/miesiąc
**Quality:** Medium (różne firmy)
**Cost:** Low (content investment)

**Actions:**
- Publish 1 deep-dive/miesiąc (AI + compliance)
- SEO for banking AI keywords
- LinkedIn posts od AI team

### Nets (Marketing) - 35% of leads

**Channels:**
- Webinary: "Sovereign AI for Banking" (miesięcznie)
- Newsletter: "Sovereign AI Insights"
- LinkedIn: Thought leadership (Tomek, AI architects)

**Volume:** 70 leads/miesiąc
**Quality:** Medium-High (engaged audience)
**Cost:** Medium (time + tools)

**Actions:**
- Monthly webinar (AI compliance)
- Bi-weekly newsletter
- LinkedIn posts o AI + banking

### Spears (Outbound ABM) - 25% of leads

**Channels:**
- Personalized research: "AI Governance Assessment dla [Bank]"
- LinkedIn direct to CISOs
- Conference networking (Banking Technology)

**Target:** TOP 30 banków w Szwajcarii i Niemczech

**Volume:** 50 leads/miesiąc
**Quality:** High (target accounts)
**Cost:** High (SDR time + research)

**Actions:**
- SDR wysyła 10 personalizowanych wiadomości/dzień (CISOs)
- Custom "AI Compliance Readiness Report" dla każdego banku
- Follow-up sequence (7 touchpoints - dłuższy cycle)

## Experiments

### Experiment 1: "Sovereign AI" vs "Private LLM"

**Hypoteza:** "Sovereign AI" przyciąga więcej banków niż "Private LLM"

**Metryka:** MQLs from banking sector

**Duration:** 1 miesiąc

**Setup:**
- 50% traffic: "Private LLM"
- 50% traffic: "Sovereign AI"

**Success:** +30% MQLs from banking

### Experiment 2: Paid vs Free Assessment

**Hypoteza:** Płatny assessment (5k EUR) kwalifikuje lepszych leadów

**Metryka:** SQL conversion rate

**Duration:** 2 miesiące

**Setup:**
- Group A: Free assessment
- Group B: 5k EUR assessment

**Success:** SQL conversion +20% dla płatnego

### Experiment 3: CISO vs CTO targeting

**Hypoteza:** Targeting CISOs daje wyższy response rate niż CTOs

**Metryka:** Response rate

**Duration:** 1 miesiąc

**Success:** +25% response rate

## ⚠️ Key Differences from CORE

| Aspekt | CORE | AI |
|--------|------|-----|
| Sales cycle | 4 tygodnie | 6 miesięcy |
| Stakeholders | 2 (CTO, CFO) | 4+ (CTO, CISO, Legal, Board) |
| Proof | Case study, trial | Assessment, POC (3m) |
| Decision | Szybsza | Wolniejsza, formalna |
| CAC | 15k PLN | 50k PLN |
| Deal size | 300k PLN | 500k PLN |
| Margin | 20% | 35% |

**Strategy:** CORE = volume, AI = margin. Obie części ważne.
